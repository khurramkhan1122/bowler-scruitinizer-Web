HOW IT WORKS

 

The application is made with an idea that is :

"To make the new bees entering in the world of bowling a glowing gem for their team"

User friendly


The application is designed in a user friendly way that anyone can use the application with a little knowledge of English.

Bowler's Bowling Action


The application analyzes the bowler's bowling action, by measuring the angle between his or her elbow and the hand, these analyzes results helps the bowler to view the angle of bowling to check that the angle is less than 15 degree as according to the ICC (International Cricket Council ) the rule for bowling is:

"A statement concerning the legality of the bowling action with respect of the amount of elbow extension measured and the 15 degree elbow extension tolerance threshold should then follow."

Angle Detection


Hence by using this application the bowler can detect the wrong angle of his or her bowling at the earliest
